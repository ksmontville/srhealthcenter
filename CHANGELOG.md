# Changelog

## November 2025 Updates

We've made several improvements to our website based on your feedback to make it easier to find the information and services you need.

### Enhanced Navigation & Accessibility
- **Patient Portal Access**: Added a convenient Patient Portal button in the sidebar for quick access from any page
- **Streamlined Navigation**: Simplified the main menu by removing duplicate Patient Portal link

### Updated Services
- **Lab Services**: Added Phlebotomy (blood draw) services to our offerings
- **Service Updates**: Removed Sports Physicals from the services list to reflect current offerings

### Improved Resources
- **Resources Section**: Reorganized and streamlined our recommended resources into clear categories with 11 carefully selected, high-quality links for healthy kids and families
- **Sidebar Title**: Updated to "Resources for Healthy Kids & Families" to better reflect the content
- **Vaccine Information**: Updated vaccine resource links to point to the most current and reliable sources, including healthychildren.org for recommended schedules

### Forms & Documentation
- **Release of Information Forms**: Separated into 4 specific forms for easier use:
  - Transferring medical records in
  - Transferring medical records out
  - School release authorization
  - Counseling records release

### School Visits Information
- **Updated Locations**: Revised school visit information to reflect current service areas
- **HealthHUB Link**: Fixed link to ensure proper access to school health resources
- **Scheduling Information**: Clarified scheduling options available through school visits

### Staff Updates
- **Welcome New Team Member**: Added Kahlee Holden to our Front Desk team

### Page Improvements
- **New Patients Page**: Improved clarity with better wording and updated terminology (now using "clinician" instead of "provider")
- **Enhanced Layout**: Increased padding in the sidebar for better readability

*We're committed to continuously improving your experience. Thank you for your feedback!*
